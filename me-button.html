The Problem

We were tasked with creating an application where the central interaction is with a button.
This button had to represent personality or character traits of a person or group of people.

Sketches

We started by sketching ideas that would solve the problem:

The Solution

We developed a practical program that simulates the spread of COVID-19 at the University of Calgary campus.
Clicking our 'me button' vaccinates a proportion of the simulated students and the results of increasing vaccinations are updated in real-time.
